Our Services

Residential Cleaning

Cleaning Sweep is committed to providing top-notch residential cleaning services tailored to meet the unique needs of every home. Our professional cleaning team ensures that every corner of your living space is spotless, using eco-friendly products and industry-leading techniques. Whether it's a one-time deep clean or regular maintenance, Clean Sweep guarantees a pristine and healthy environment for you and your family. We take pride in delivering meticulous attention to detail, ensuring your home not only looks clean but feels fresh and inviting. Trust Clean Sweep to transform your home into a space of comfort and cleanliness.

Commercial Cleaning

At Cleaning Sweep, we specialize in delivering exceptional commercial cleaning services that create a safe, sanitary, and productive work environment. Our highly trained cleaning professionals are equipped with state-of-the-art tools and eco-friendly products to ensure your office, retail space, or commercial facility is cleaned to the highest standards. We understand the importance of maintaining a clean workspace to enhance employee morale and make a great impression on clients. From routine cleaning to specialized services like deep cleaning and disinfection, Clean Sweep offers flexible and reliable solutions tailored to the specific needs of your business.

Special Cleaning

Cleaning Sweep offers specialized cleaning services designed to tackle the most challenging tasks, including carpet cleaning, window washing, and upholstery cleaning. Our professional team uses advanced equipment and proven techniques to remove deep-seated dirt, stains, and allergens from carpets, leaving them fresh and revitalized. We also provide thorough window washing services, ensuring your windows are spotless and streak-free, allowing natural light to shine through. For upholstery, we use gentle yet effective cleaning methods to restore the beauty and comfort of your furniture, extending its lifespan. Trust Clean Sweep to handle these detailed cleaning jobs with precision and care, making every aspect of your home or business look immaculate.

ABOUT US

Founded in 2024 by Carlo Sajonia, Cleaning Sweep started with a reliable, high-quality cleaning services that prioritize customer satisfaction and attention to detail. Initially serving a small community, the company quickly expanded through word-of-mouth recommendations. Clean Sweep embraced eco-friendly products and innovative techniques, offering tailored cleaning solutions for both residential and commercial clients. Despite its growth, Clean Sweep remains committed to its core values of trust, integrity, and personalized service. Today, it continues to deliver spotless, inviting environments, ensuring peace of mind with every clean, and setting a high standard in the industry.
